Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: November 7, 2025

1. Overview

ETHENIC CHIC is primarily a fashion manufacturer supplying high-quality garments. We sell a selection of manufactured products directly through this website. Our priority is product quality and customer satisfaction.

2. Demo / Early Launch Policy

During demo or pre-launch stages, some products or offers may be provided for preview only. In such cases, returns may not be available. Where returns are unavailable we will clearly indicate that on the product page.

3. Standard Return Policy (When Applicable)
  • Returns are accepted within 7 days from delivery only if the product is defective or materially different from its description.
  • Products must be unused, with original tags and packaging (where applicable).
  • Return shipping costs may be borne by the customer unless the return is due to our error.
  • Inspection will be carried out on receipt — refunds are processed within 7–10 business days after successful inspection.
4. How to Request a Return
  1. Contact our support at support@ethinicchic.com or use the Contact page with your order details.
  2. Provide order number, product name, photos of the issue (if defective), and a short description.
  3. We’ll respond within 48 hours with return instructions.
5. Refund Method

Refunds will be credited to the original payment method where possible. If payment gateway refund is not feasible, we may issue store credit or a bank transfer as agreed.

6. Exceptions & Non-Returnable Items
  • Products damaged after delivery due to misuse are not eligible.
  • Made-to-order or customized/manufactured-to-spec items may not be returnable unless there is a manufacturing defect.
  • Clearance or “final sale” items will be marked and are not returnable.
7. Contact

If you have any questions about returns, email support@ethinicchic.com or message us on WhatsApp at +91 7994334260.
